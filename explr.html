Explr.fm

This project was made in the course Information Visaualisation at KTH. Explr.fm is a visualisation of where the music you listen to comes from. The data comes from last.fm. It is made from the last.fm API and javascript linrary d3.js. The idea come from us wanting to visualize the music we have listened to as a journey.

When using explr.fm you start by writing in your last.fm username.
