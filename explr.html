Explr.fm

This project was made in the course Information Visualization at KTH. Explr.fm is a visualization of where the music you listen to comes from. It is made from the last.fm. API and the javascript library d3.js. We wanted to visualize music listening as a journey.

When using explr.fm you start by writing in your last.fm user name. Mine is piramide556 if you don't have one.

You get details if you zoom in and click on a country. You will see your top artists.
