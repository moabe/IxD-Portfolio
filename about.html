Hi! My name is Moa Bergsmark and I study at the masters program in human computer interaction at KTH in Stockholm. You have found your way to my portfolio, here I show student projects I have been part of.

Right now I am doing my masters thesis with the Interaction design team at the department for media technology and interaction design. In my thesis I am exploring designing for children's cooperative play with mobile phones on the floor, interacting with NFC-tags ( see first little working prototype ).

In the future I want to work as an interaction designer and make playful interactions where the physical and digital world intermix.
